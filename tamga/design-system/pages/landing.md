# Landing Page Overrides

## Structure and Spacing

- Hero section should be full-width and visually dominant.
- Use generous whitespace with clear section rhythm.
- Section spacing:
  - desktop: `6rem`
  - mobile: `4rem`

## Typography and CTA

- Use bold headline treatment in hero.
- CTA color must come from `../MASTER.md` accent/CTA (`#0369A1`).

## Terminal Mockup

- Terminal block must always remain dark (independent from page theme).
- Use monospace typography for commands and logs.

## Anti-Patterns for this page

- Avoid generic stock photos.
- Do not exceed 8 major sections.
- Do not use emoji as icons.
